Ingredients: 2 chicken breasts, 4 cups mixed greens, 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, 1/4 cup sliced cucumbers, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar, salt & pepper.

Instructions: Grill chicken until cooked through. Slice and toss with greens, tomatoes, and cucumbers. Whisk olive oil, balsamic, salt, and pepper for dressing.
